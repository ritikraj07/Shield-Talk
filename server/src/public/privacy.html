At Shield-Talk, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Technical Information: We collect information about your device, browser, and connection to help us optimize our service.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our services to improve user experience.
  • Reported Content: If you report a conversation, we collect the reported content for moderation purposes.

2. What We Don't Collect

To protect your privacy:

  • We do not store the content of your conversations after the chat ends
  • We do not record audio or video chats
  • We do not require your real name or personal details
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Protect our users and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Send you important information about our services

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect against legal liability
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and the public
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All conversations are end-to-end encrypted
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your account information
  • We regularly review our information collection and processing practices

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Access and update your account information
  • Delete your account at any time
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Use our services without creating an account (with limited features)

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@shield-talk.com.
